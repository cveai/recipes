Ingredients:
 - 2 bowls of steamed rice
 - kimchi, chopped, proper amount
 - hams, chopped
 - butter
 - 쯔유

Directions:
 1. Put butter in a sizzling pan.
 2. Add treamed rice, chopped kinchi and hams.
 3. Cook for a bout 5 mins, stirring occasionally. 
 4. Put 물로 희석된 쯔유 on the rice.
 5. Cook for another 2 mins.
 6. Enjoy!
